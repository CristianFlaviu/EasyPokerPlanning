# UX Review: Full Product

## Goal

Inspect the whole app as a first-time user and propose practical v1 UX/design improvements.

## Read First

- `docs/ai-ux-review/review-guide.md`
- `CLAUDE.md`
- `AGENTS.md`
- `frontend/CLAUDE.md`
- `docs/domain-model.md`
- `docs/progress.md`

## Tooling

Use the Browser plugin/skill. Do not change code.

## Review Steps

1. Open the lobby.
2. Review first impression, visual hierarchy, and create-room clarity.
3. Create a room as owner.
4. Review the room page before a round starts.
5. Start a round and review voting state.
6. Vote and review selected-vote feedback.
7. Reveal votes and review results/statistics presentation.
8. End the round and review transition back to waiting state.
9. Open history and review completed-round discoverability.
10. Repeat key screens at a narrow mobile viewport.

## Output Required

Produce:

- Overall UX score from 1-10 with a short rationale.
- Top 5 friction points.
- Top 5 proposed improvements.
- One recommended v1 polish slice that can be implemented in one session.
- One larger design direction for later.
- Screenshots or precise page-state notes for important observations.
